    Manufacturing Process of Copper

    Copper is typically extracted from oxide and sulfide ores that contain between 0.5 and 2.0 percent copper.

    Regardless of the ore type, mined copper ore must first be concentrated to remove gangue or unwanted materials embedded in the ore.
